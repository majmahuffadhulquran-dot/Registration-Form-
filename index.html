📜 Programme Rules & Regulations

Before proceeding to the registration form, you are required to carefully read and understand the following rules and regulations governing the Majma' Huffāẓ al-Qur'an Programme. Enrollment signifies your full acceptance of and commitment to these terms.

1 Sincerity of Intention (Ikhlāṣ)
All participants must join with a sincere intention for the sake of Allah alone. The programme is an act of 'ibādah, not a social activity.
2 Punctuality and Attendance
  • Students must attend all scheduled sessions on time.
  • Absence without a valid excuse may lead to warnings or removal from the programme.
  • Consistent lateness will not be tolerated.
3 Commitment to Assigned Track
  • Each student must remain committed to their chosen track — whether Hifdh, Murāja'ah, Tafseer, or Qirā'āt.
  • Daily or weekly targets must be met as assigned by the instructor.
4 Dress Code and Conduct
  • Students must dress modestly in accordance with Islamic guidelines.
  • Respectful behaviour is mandatory at all times.
  • Any form of misconduct, disrespect, or disturbance will not be accepted.
5 Use of Phones and Distractions
  • Phones must be on silent during sessions.
  • Unnecessary use of devices during class is prohibited unless permitted for learning purposes.
6 Academic Discipline
  • Students must come prepared for every session.
  • Failure to revise previous portions or complete assignments will affect progress and may lead to disciplinary action.
7 Qur'an Etiquette (Adab al-Qur'an)
  • Participants must observe proper etiquette with the Qur'an, including cleanliness, focus, and respect.
  • No careless handling or recitation is permitted.
8 Health and Honesty
  • Students must truthfully declare any health conditions that may affect participation.
  • The organisation will not be held responsible for undisclosed conditions.
9 Obedience to Instructors
  • Students must respect and follow the guidance of instructors and coordinators at all times.
  • Corrections must be accepted with humility.
10 Withdrawal and Dismissal
  • Any student who fails to meet the standards of the programme may be warned, suspended, or removed.
  • Voluntary withdrawal must be communicated properly to the organisers.
11 Participation in Activities
Students may be required to participate in Qur'an-related events, assessments, or group activities organised by the programme.
12 Representation of the Programme
All participants are ambassadors of Majma' Huffāẓ. Their character must reflect the dignity of the Qur'an both within and outside the programme environment.
Joining this programme is a commitment to growth, discipline, and devotion to the Qur'an.
Only serious and dedicated students are encouraged to proceed.
